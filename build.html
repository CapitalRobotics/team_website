The Build Process

Explore the engineering behind every component of our robot.

Chassis

Foundation: The chassis is the backbone of our robot, providing stability and housing other components. Built with GoBilda’s high-strength aluminum channels, it withstands the rigors of competition.

Features: Our design includes a four-wheel drive system with omni wheels for agile movement, allowing swift directional changes without losing traction.

Challenge Solved: Using GoBilda components helped simplify our chassis assembly, enabling us to build a robust structure quickly and efficiently.

Claw

Design: The claw is custom-built using GoBilda servo mounts for precise and stable control, giving us accuracy in gripping and releasing game elements.

Mechanics: Equipped with a dual servo mechanism, our claw can adjust grip strength and opening angle dynamically based on sensor feedback.

Impact: The claw’s reliability has significantly improved our ability to handle delicate objects smoothly, minimizing the risk of dropping or damaging elements.

Arm

Construction: The arm uses GoBilda’s telescoping system, giving us the reach and flexibility to interact with elevated game elements effectively.

Range and Control: Our arm extends to nearly double its retracted length and incorporates an encoder for precise positional feedback, allowing fine control.

Future Plans: We’re considering adding a counterbalance system for smoother movements and even greater control during fast-paced matches.

Materials Used

High-strength aluminum, carbon fiber, and other durable materials ensure a lightweight yet robust build.

Assembly Process

Our team meticulously assembled each part to ensure seamless integration, focusing on modularity for easy repairs.

Testing & Optimization

Rigorous testing helped optimize each component for stability, speed, and control.

GoBilda Components

Leveraging GoBilda parts allows us to maintain reliability and compatibility across various modules.
